About FieldBridge HQ

From the job site to the trailer — we help construction teams stop chasing RFIs.

Who We Are

FieldBridge HQ is a construction workflow practice founded by a Senior Construction Project Manager with years of experience on Ontario high-rise and commercial projects. We've run RFI logs, managed submittal registers, and stood in the trailer when a coordination issue stopped the pour.

After years of watching RFIs eat schedules, submittals stall projects, and coordination overhead drain profitability, we recognized that the industry's biggest problem isn't a lack of technology — it's workflow design. The tools exist. The playbooks don't.

FieldBridge HQ exists to write those playbooks.

What We Do

We help construction project teams cut RFI cycles without technical setup. We audit your current workflow, identify the bottlenecks, and deliver a system that gets RFIs answered in days instead of weeks.

We don't sell software. We don't ask you to install anything. Every template and workflow works with the tools you already use — email, Procore, Microsoft Office.

Our Approach

  • Practitioner-grade — Every workflow was developed by people who have done this work. No theoretical frameworks from people who've never touched a spec book.
  • No tech skills required — We handle the setup. You get templates and workflows that work with the tools you already have.
  • ROI-backed — Every recommendation is tied to measurable cycle-time reduction. We show you the math before you invest.
  • Deployable this week — No 6-month digital transformation. You can start seeing faster RFI cycles in days, not months.

Why Now

Construction productivity has been flat for 50 years while every other industry has found ways to cut coordination waste. The gap isn't technology — it's translation. Turning what's possible into what a PM can actually use without a technical background.

That's what FieldBridge HQ bridges.
