About FieldBridge HQ

From the job site to AI — bridging the gap that costs the industry $31B/year.

Who We Are

FieldBridge HQ is a construction technology practice founded by Fadi Felfel, a Senior Construction Project Manager with deep experience on Ontario high-rise and commercial projects.

After years of watching RFIs eat schedules, submittals stall projects, and coordination overhead drain profitability, Fadi recognized that the industry's biggest problem isn't technology access — it's workflow design. The tools exist. The playbooks don't.

FieldBridge HQ exists to write those playbooks.

What We Do

We build and deploy AI-powered workflow automation for construction project management teams. Our focus is the coordination layer — RFIs, submittals, spec compliance, schedule communication — where 86% of the time spent is non-value-adding friction.

We don't sell software licenses. We sell implementation: the prompts, the workflows, the audit templates, and the change management that turns AI tools into measurable cycle-time reduction on active projects.
